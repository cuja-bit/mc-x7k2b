How to read this map

X — RS-Ratio: the sector's trend of relative strength vs NIFTY 500 (100 = matching the index; right of 100 = outperforming trend). Y — RS-Momentum: the rate of change of that relative strength (above 100 = the outperformance is accelerating).

IMPROVING ↖weak but gaining — early accumulation candidates
LEADING ↗strong & rising — fish here first
LAGGING ↙weak & fading — avoid / short study
WEAKENING ↘still strong, losing steam — tighten, take profits

Sectors orbit clockwise: Improving → Leading → Weakening → Lagging. The comet tail shows the path — each segment keeps the colour of the quadrant it passed through, so you can read the whole journey at a glance. Dots along the tail are one bar apart: wider spacing = faster rotation.
